About Me

I am a Masters student (expected May 2023) in Computational Science in the Department of Computational and Data Sciences (CDS) at George Mason University. I received my Bachelor's degree from George Mason University in December 2018 with double-majors in CDS and in Neuroscience.

My interests (reflected in my coursework) include machine learning, statistical inference, and data science.
